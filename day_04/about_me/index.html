About Me

I grew up in New Orleans, La and moved to Austin 4 years ago. I love my heritage and try to cook and bake as much from my Maw Maw's cookbooks as possible. I also frequent this site (Lottie + Doof) for new recipes and other inspirations.In college I studied visual art, and have a deep love of the creative process. For this reason, I knew I needed to find a path that allowed me to live the way I want to with my friends and family, while staying engaged in that process. Cheers to learning and Web Design!
